My Works

Canfield Consortium serves East Canfield Village, an underserved neighborhood in the Detroit area. Over the summer, East Canfield Village suffers from chronic flooding. We worked with Canfield Consortium to improve communication during flood time.

The transition from an iOS to Android operating system can be difficult. This project focused on understanding that experience and identifying Google Voice Assistant's potential to assist users in this onboarding experience.

Maize & Blue Cupboard is an on-campus food pantry for budget-tight university students. This organization works with their Feeding America partner, Food Gatherers, to receive food donations from volunteers, donors, and local farms. Maize & Blue has been working to revolutionize the language surrounding food insecurity - they are working to dissolve the negative connotations associated with food insecurity and provide a positive, supportive community with empathy and compassion.
